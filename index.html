Our story

Plan is an independent product strategy consultancy. Based in London, we help world-leading mobility and consumer tech companies navigate the tricky early stages of innovation.

We’re a team of researchers, strategists and designers. Since we set up in 2004, we’ve made a name for cutting through complexity and offering clear, independent and friendly advice.
